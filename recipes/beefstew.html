Beef Stew

Follow these instructions to make the best most delicious beef stew in the world!

Ingredients

  • 10 pounds of beef chuck roast
  • 2 tons of beef broth
  • 5 pounds each of carrots and potatos

Steps

  1. cook the beef with onions
  2. pour in the broth with wine
  3. boil then bring to a simmer
