Oatmeal

Follow these instructions for the best most delicious oatmeal in the world!

Ingredients

  • 50 pounds of oatmeal
  • 1000 pounds of mixed fruits, ideally strawberries and bananas
  • 10 tons of honey
  • 50 tons of peanut butter

Steps

  1. Cook the oatmeal until edible
  2. Toss fruits into oatmeal bowl
  3. Slam peanut butter onto the cooked oatmeal and fruit mix
  4. Drizzle honey on top of the delicious oatmeal creation. Enjoy!
