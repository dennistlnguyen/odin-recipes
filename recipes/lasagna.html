Lasagna

Follow these instructions to make the best most delicious lasagna in the world!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of pasta
  • 50 pounds of green cheese
  • 6 tons of marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta rectangles
  2. Layer the pasta rectangles such that that green cheese and marinara sauce are sandwiched between the pasta rectanbles
  3. Garnish with parmesan cheese and cilantro. Enjoy!
